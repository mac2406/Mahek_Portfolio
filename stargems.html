StarGems Inc.
Lumina Vault Suite
Project Overview

Designed and implemented the Lumina Vault dashboard suite for StarGems Inc., streamlining complex diamond and jewelry management workflows. The project involved creating a high-end, professional interface for pricing, inventory control, and market analysis.

VISIT LIVE SITE ↗
01 / Problem

The Challenge

StarGems Inc. managed their diamond inventory, markup calculations, and rate tracking across disconnected spreadsheets and manual records. This created errors in pricing, delays in market response, and no unified view for administrators. The business needed a cohesive digital suite to bring clarity to complex multi-module workflows.

02 / Process

How I Worked

Research & Discovery

Conducted stakeholder interviews to understand the pricing workflows, diamond grading terminology, and pain points in the existing system.

Information Architecture

Mapped all modules — Markup Management, Rate Tracking, Stone Pricing, and the central Dashboard — into a coherent navigation hierarchy.

Iterative Design

Built low-fidelity wireframes in Figma, then progressed to high-fidelity prototypes with client feedback loops at each milestone.

03 / Solution

The Deliverables

Markup Management

A sophisticated tool for managing retail and wholesale markups with real-time calculations and bulk update capabilities.

MODULE 01 PRICING

Rate Tracking System

Integrated metal and stone market rates tracking with automated margin adjustments for precision pricing.

MODULE 02 METRICS

Stone Pricing Explorer

An interactive search and comparison tool for evaluating stone prices based on cut, color, clarity, and carat weight.

MODULE 03 EXPLORER

Main Dashboard

The central hub for administrators, providing high-level insights and quick access to all management modules.
