01 / Problem

The Challenge

StarGems Inc. managed their diamond inventory, markup calculations, and rate tracking across disconnected spreadsheets and manual records. This created errors in pricing, delays in market response, and no unified view for administrators. The business needed a cohesive digital suite to bring clarity to complex multi-module workflows.

02 / Process

How I Worked

Research & Discovery

Conducted stakeholder interviews to understand the pricing workflows, diamond grading terminology, and pain points in the existing system.

Information Architecture

Mapped all modules — Markup Management, Rate Tracking, Stone Pricing, and the central Dashboard — into a coherent navigation hierarchy.

Iterative Design

Built low-fidelity wireframes in Figma, then progressed to high-fidelity prototypes with client feedback loops at each milestone.
