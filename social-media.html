Inkvise
Brand Identity Design

Overview:

Designed the visual identity for Inkvise, a brand dedicated to redefining the art of writing with elegance and purpose for modern professionals. The identity balances classic authority with minimalist simplicity.

The process started from conceptual pencil sketches on notebooks, building up to a structured wordmark where "ink meets precision." The visual palette relies on deep blue, slate, and crisp white typography.

Anyhow Media
Campaign Poster Design

Overview:

Developed high-impact digital campaign graphics for Anyhow Media. Under the theme "Imagine a world without...", the posts use strong typography, bold layouts, and textured red overlays to draw viewer attention instantly.

Focusing on corporate branding and culture statements like "Your Culture is Your Brand" and "Seconds matter, make it count," the campaign is designed for high-conversion social feeds.

Starbucks

Designed a continuous 10-panel carousel ribbon for Starbucks. The layout allows each post to merge seamlessly into the next, maintaining visual continuity as the user swishes through the slides on their feed.
